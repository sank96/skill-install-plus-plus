# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

Examples of positive behavior:

- giving constructive technical feedback
- assuming good intent while staying precise
- focusing criticism on ideas and code, not people
- welcoming contributors with different levels of experience

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- intentionally disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions
that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
through the repository owner account on GitHub.
